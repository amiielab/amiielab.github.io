AI Webinar Series

We present the PACE AI Insights Series, a cutting-edge webinar series aimed at creating a formal platform for scientists, students, and practitioners of artificial intelligence (AI). The goal of this series is to establish a professional forum for sharing innovative research and development in AI.

  • Apr 18, 2025 [11:00 am EDT]: "Unlocking the Power of AI", Khalifeh AlJadda, PhD, Director of Data Science at Google and Founder of Optimized AI Conference. [Registration (Free)]

Our mission through IEEE Women in Computing (WIC) is to foster a diverse and inclusive community that empowers and supports women in computational sciences, including AI and data science. We promote professional development, networking opportunities, and leadership skills for women at all career stages.

Our vision is to build a future where women in computing lead and significantly contribute to advancing computational strategies through innovation, creativity, and leadership.

With my affiliation as leading personnel at IEEE WIC, Atlanta section, I organized the Women in Computing Webinar Series at the University of Georgia.
