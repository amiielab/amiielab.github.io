Openings

PhD Positions

I am continuously seeking self-motivated PhD students with a strong background in AI and medical image analysis. However, I only recruit PhD students from the graduate programs at Pace University. Therefore, if you are a prospective doctoral student, please apply directly to the appropriate graduate program at Pace, and feel free to contact me once you have been admitted. I also encourage interested applicants to explore my AMIIE Laboratory to learn more about our ongoing research activities.

MSc Positions

If you are an MSc student at Pace University and are interested in conducting your Capstone Project under my supervision or joining my Pace AMIIE Laboratory, please email me your CV and a summary of your research interests. For those not yet enrolled at Pace and looking for funded MSc positions, please be aware that I do not have any available funding opportunities for MSc applicants at this time.
